\begin{definition}[Big Zariski site]\label[definition]{79d}
Define the big Zariski site 
to be $\rsite{\schemes}{T}{O}$ with the following components.
The underlying category is $\schemes$.
The topology $T$ is generated by 
the basis cosisting of 
the covering families $\set{X_i \xrightarrow{f_i} X}$
where $f_i$ is an open immersion and $\Union_i f_i(X_i) = X$.
The sheaf of rings $\sheaf{O}$ sends $\rspace{U}{Q} \rightarrow \rspace{X}{O}$
to $\sect{U}{Q}$.

We will mostly be interested in the sliced site $\overschemes{X}$ for a scheme $X$.
\end{definition}
