\begin{lemma}\label[lemma]{acc}
Let $X$ be a scheme.
Consider $k: \opens{X} \rightarrow \overschemes{X}$.
Then $\direct{k}\stilde = \stilde$
\end{lemma}
\begin{proof}
By definition $k^{\#}$ is an isomorphism.
By \Cref{ac1} we get that $k$ is cover lifting.
Then we can apply $\Cref{a2f}$.
Let $\sheaf{U}$ be the structure sheaf of $\overschemes{X}$.
Let $\sheaf{O}$ be the structure sheaf of $\opens{X}$.
Note that the structure morphism $\sect{\terminal}{\direct{k}U} \rightarrow \gblsect{O}$
is an isomorphism, so \Cref{a2f} implies this result.
\end{proof}
