\begin{definition}[inverse image of presheaves]\label[definition]{4bd}
Let $\cat{C}, \cat{D}$ be a categories.
Let $f: \cat{C} \rightarrow \cat{D}$ be a functor.
Define the inverse image functor
$\inverseimg{f}: \presheaves{C} \rightarrow \presheaves{D}$
as follows.
Let $\presheaf{F} \in \presheaves{C}$.
For any $d \in \cat{D}$ 
\[\inverseimg{f}(F)(d) = \colim{\overcat{D}{d}}{\presheaf{F}u} .\]
\end{definition}
