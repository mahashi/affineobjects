\begin{definition}[direct image]\label[definition]{4b5}
Let $f : \cat{C}\rightarrow \cat{D}$.
Define the direct image functor 
$\direct{f}: \presheaves{D} \rightarrow \presheaves{C}$
as \[ \direct{f} = \blank \circ f.\]

When $u$ is the forgetful functor $\overcat{C}{a} \rightarrow C$
then we denote $\direct{u}$ sometimes by $\restr{a}{\psh{F}}$.
\end{definition}
