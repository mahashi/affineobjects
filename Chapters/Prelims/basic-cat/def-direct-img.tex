\begin{definition}[direct image]\label[definition]{570}
Let $f : \cat{C}\rightarrow \cat{D}$.
Define the direct image functor 
$\direct{f}: \presheaves{D} \rightarrow \presheaves{C}$
as \[ \direct{f} = \blank \circ f.\]

We call $\direct{f}\psh{F}$ the restriction of $\psh{F}$ to $\cat{C}$
When $f$ is the induced functor $\overcat{C}{b} \rightarrow \overcat{C}{a}$
coming from a morphism $g:b\rightarrow a$
the we sometimes denote it by $\restr{b}{\psh{F}}$.
\end{definition}
