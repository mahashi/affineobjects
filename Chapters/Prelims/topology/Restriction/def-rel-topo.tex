\begin{definition}[Relative topology]\label[definition]{a80}
Let $\site{C}{T}$ be a site.
Let $a \in \cat{C}$.

Define the induced topology $\top{T}_a$ on $\overcat{C}{a}$
by, for each $f \in \overcat{C}{a}$
\[\top{T}_a(f) = \top{T}(\dom{f}).\]

The identification from \Cref{b17} implies that $\top{T}_a$ is a Grothendieck topology.
\end{definition}
