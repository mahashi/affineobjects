\begin{definition}\label[definition]{a62}
Let $\cat{C}$ be a category.
Let $a\in \cat{C}$.
Assume that all pullbacks exist in $\cat{C}$.

Then we get a right adjoint of $u:\overcat{C}{a} \rightarrow \cat{C}$.
Namely, $v: C \rightarrow \overcat{C}{a}$, defined by:

for each $b\in \cat{C}$
\[ v(b) = (b\times a, \mbox{pr}_a),\]
where $\mbox{pr}_a$ is the projection onto $a$.
For each $f:c\rightarrow b \in \cat{C}$
\[ v(f) = f \times \Id_a.\]
\end{definition}
