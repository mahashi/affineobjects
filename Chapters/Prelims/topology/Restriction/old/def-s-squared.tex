\begin{definition}[$s^2$]\label[definition]{a72}
Define 
\[s^2: \direct{u} \circ \plus \circ \plus \rightarrow \plus \circ \plus \circ \direct{u}\]
as
\[s^2_{\presheaf{F}} = \plus{s_{\presheaf{F}}} \circ s_{\plus{\presheaf{F}}}.\]

This is a natural isomorphism because $s$ is one.
Hence sheafifying and restricting commute via this iso
\[s^2: \direct{u} \circ \sheafify_a \rightarrow \sheafify_b \circ \direct{u} .\]
\end{definition}
