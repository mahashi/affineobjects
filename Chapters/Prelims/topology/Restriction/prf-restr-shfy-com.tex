\begin{corollary}
Let $Y = \site{C}{T}$.
Let $a,b \in \cat{C}$.
Let $f: b\rightarrow a$.
Sheafifying and restricting commute via the iso
\[s^2: \sheafify_b \circ \restrictOpt{b} \rightarrow \restrictOpt{b} \circ \sheafify_a.\]
\end{corollary}
