\begin{definition}[inverse image of sheaves]\label[definition]{ac5}
Let $\site{C}{T}, \site{D}{S}$ be sites.
Let $f: \cat{C} \rightarrow \cat{D}$ be a morphism of sites.
Define the inverse image functor 
$\inverseimg{f}: \sheaves{C} \rightarrow \sheaves{D}$
of sheaves to be
\[\shs{C} \xrightarrow{\inverseimg{f}} \pshs{D} \xrightarrow{\sheafify} \shs{C}.\]
the inverse image of presheaves composed with sheafification.
\end{definition}
