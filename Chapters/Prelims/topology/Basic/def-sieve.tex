\begin{definition}[Sieve]\label[definition]{Sieve}
Let $\cat{C}$ be a category 
and $a \in \cat{C}$.
Define the maximal sieve $\maxsieve{a}$ on $a$ to be the collection of all morphisms to $a$.
In formula,
\[\maxsieve{a} = \set{ f \in \cat{C} \suchthat \codomain{f} = a}.\]

A sieve $S$ on $a$ is a subcollection of $\maxsieve{a}$ such that 
$gf \in S$ for any $f\in S$ and any $g$.
\end{definition}
