\begin{definition}[cocontinuous functor]\label[definition]{be9}
The functor $G$ is said to lift covers or be cocontinuous
if for every $R\in \cov{G(c)}$ there is some $S\in \cov{c}$ 
such that $G(S)\subset R$.
See \cite[\href{http://stacks.math.columbia.edu/tag/00XJ}{Tag 00XJ}]{stacks}.
\end{definition}
