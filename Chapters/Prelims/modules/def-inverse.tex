\begin{definition}[inverse image of modules]\label[definition]{910}
Let $\site{C}{T}{O}, \site{D}{S}{U}$ be ringed sites.
Let $f: \cat{C} \rightarrow \cat{D}$ be a morphism of ringed sites.
Define the inverse image functor
$\inverseimg{f}: \shmod{O} \rightarrow \shmod{U}$
of modules to be
\[\shmod{O} \xrightarrow{\inverseimg{f}} \shmod{\inverseimg{f}O} \xrightarrow{} \shmod{U}.\]
the inverse image of sheaves composed with tensoring with $\sheaf{U}$
along the map $f^{\#}:\inverseimg{f}O \rightarrow U$.
\end{definition}
