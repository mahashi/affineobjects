\begin{remark}[$\pstilde$ counit commute with restriction]\label[remark]{842}
Let $\rsite{C}{T}{O}$ be a ringed site.
Let $M$ a $\sect{1}{O}$-module.
Let $\sheaf{G} =\pstilde{a}{M\tensor \sect{a}{O}}$

Note that $\counit_{\pstilde}$ is the identity transformation. Same holds for $\counit_a$.
So $\inverse{t}_{\sheaf{G}} \circ \direct{u}\counit_{\pstilde{M}}\circ t_{\sheaf{G}} = \id$ too.
In short form this can be expressed as
\[\inverse{t} \circ \direct{u} \counit \circ t
	= \counit_{a,\pstilde{\blank\tensor\sect{a}{O}}}.\]
\end{remark}
