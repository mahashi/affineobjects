\begin{definition}\label[definition]{966}
Let $\rsite{C}{T}{O}$ be a ringed site.
Define
\[\stilde: \modules{R} \rightarrow \shmodules{O}\]
by
$\sheafify \circ \pstilde$.

It follows by that we have the adjunction $\adj{\stilde}{\globalsections}$.

This functor is the generalisation of \cite[\href{http://stacks.math.columbia.edu/tag/01BH}{Tag 01BH}]{stacks} to general sites,
so $\stilde$ on $\opens{X}$ for some ringed space $X$
will coincide with the construction defined in the Stacks Project.
\end{definition}
