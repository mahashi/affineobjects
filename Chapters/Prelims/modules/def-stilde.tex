\begin{definition}\label[definition]{789}
Let $\rsite{C}{T}{O}$
Define
\[\stilde: \modules{R} \rightarrow \shmodules{O}\]
by
$\sheafify \circ \pstilde$.

It follows that we have the adjunction $\adj{\stilde}{\globalsections}$.
This functor is generalisation of \cite[Tag 01BH]{stacks}.
\end{definition}
