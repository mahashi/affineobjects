\begin{definition}\label[definition]{759}
Let $\rsite{C}{T}{O}$ be a ringed site.
Define
\[\stilde: \modules{R} \rightarrow \shmodules{O}\]
by
$\sheafify \circ \pstilde$.

It follows that we have the adjunction $\adj{\stilde}{\globalsections}$.

This functor is the generalisation of \cite[Tag 01BH]{stacks} to general sites,
so $\stilde$ on the site corresponding to a ringed space(we will define this later)
will coincide with the construction defined in the Stacks Project.
\end{definition}
