\begin{lemma}[$\stilde$ commutes with restriction]\label[lemma]{78a}
Let $\rsite{C}{T}{O}$ be a ringed site.
Let $f:\site{C}{T} \rightarrow \site{D}{S}$
be a morphism of sites that lift covers.
Let $f^{\#}$ be an isomorphism.

We have a natural isomorphism  
\[\direct{f} \circ \stilde \Rightarrow \stilde \circ (\blank \tensor_{\sect{1}{O}} \sections{1}{U}).\]
\end{lemma}

\begin{proof}
Follows from \Cref{769,8e0}.
\end{proof}
