\begin{definition}[Tilde functor]\label[definition]{910}
Let $\Spec{R}$ be an affine scheme.
Let $M$ be a $R$-module.
Define $\widetilde{M}$ to be the unique sheaf on $\Spec{R}$ with
\[
\wt{M}:D(f) \mapsto M_f.
\]
See \cite[Tag 01HR]{stacks}.
\end{definition}

\begin{remark}\label[remark]{91a}
This functor(with this notation) is commonly used in algebraic geometry texts.
We will show that it is equal to $\stilde$ on the small Zariski site and then only use $\stilde$.
\end{remark}
