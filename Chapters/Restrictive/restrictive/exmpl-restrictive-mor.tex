\begin{example}\label[example]{cc5}
In $\overschemes{\Spec{A}}$
the morphism $\Spec{A_f} \rightarrow \Spec{A}$ is restrictive.
Let $\sheaf{G}$ be a quasi-coherent sheaf on $\overschemes{\Spec{A}}$.
This implies that $\sheaf{G} = \stilde{\sect{\Spec{A}}{G}}$, see ?.
The morphism
\[
\sections{\Spec{A}}{G} \tensor_{\gblsect{O}} \sections{\Spec{A_f}}{O} \rightarrow  \sections{\Spec{A_f}}{G} = \sections{A}{G}_f,
\]
\[ m \tensor r \rightarrow rm\]
is an isomorphism by basic commutative algebra.
\end{example}
