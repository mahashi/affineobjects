\begin{definition}\label[definition]{c89}
Let $\rsite{C}{T}{O}$.
A family of morphisms $f_i: a_i \rightarrow b_i \in \cat{C}$ is called a restrictive family 
if a sheaf module $\sheaf{G}$ on $\overcat{C}{b}$ is quasi-coherent \iff the morphisms
$$
\widehat{f_i}: \sect{b_i}{G} \tensor_{\sect{b_i}{O}} \sect{a_i}{O} \rightarrow \sect{a_i}{G}
$$
are isomorphism.
\end{definition}
