\begin{example}\label[example]{501}
Let $\cat{C}$ be as in \Cref{4c7}.
Consider $G = \sheaf{O}(i,j)[y_{k,l}| k\leq i \; \& \; l \leq j]$. 
Let $\coproduct_{k\in I} \sheaf{O} \xrightarrow{\alpha} G$ be any sheaf map.
Let $\alpha(e_k)$ be the image of the generators $e_k\in \coproduct_{k\in I} \sheaf{O}$ in the global sections. 
The section $y_{1,1}\in G(1,1)$ cannot be written 
as a finite sum $\sum_k \lambda_k \alpha(e_k)$ 
for scalars $\lambda_k\in \sheaf{O}(i,j)$ for any $(i,j)$.
This shows that $\alpha$ is not surjective hence $G$ is not quasi-coherent(locally presentable).
\end{example}
